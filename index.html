🔥 Warm-Up & Cool-Down

Pre-Workout Warm-Up (8–10 mins)

Exercise Duration/Reps Notes
Arm Circles (forward/backward) 20 sec each Gradually increase circle size
Jumping Jacks 30 sec Light cardio to boost heart rate
Cat-Cow Stretch 10 reps Mobilize the spine
Hip Circles 10 each side Enhance hip joint flexibility
World's Greatest Stretch 5 reps/side Combine thoracic and hip mobility
Glute Bridges 15 reps Activate glutes, protect lower back
Inchworm Walkouts 5 reps Engage core and stretch hamstrings
Band Pull-Aparts or Wall Angels 15 reps Activate upper back, correct posture

Post-Workout Cool-Down (10–12 mins)

  • Child’s pose
  • Cobra stretch
  • Figure-4 stretch
  • Breathing exercises (box breathing or nasal breathing)

✅ Workout Safety & Tips

  • No deadlifts for first 4 weeks
  • 60 min max per session
  • Posture drills: 2x/week
  • HIIT: incline walks to start
